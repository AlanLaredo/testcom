Fast & easy!

Intermediate Level

-Deep frying quick guide
Homemade style

Safety equipment

Apron / Heat resistant gloves

You will need:

Deep Fryer
Cooking oil
Thermometer
Seasonings (optional)
Palmex® Pellets
Plastic bags for packing

  • Instructions

  • 1.Fill the deep fryer with the cooking oil to manufacturers recommended level
  • 2.Heat the cooking oil at the temperature recommended (see our frying manual)
  • 3.Add a few amount of Palmex® Pellets in the deep fryer
  • 4.Stir slowly with a ladle for 10 to 15 seconds until the Snacks look puffed
  • 5.Carefully remove the Snacks with a strainer and shake it up slowly to remove excess oil
  • 6.Allow the Snacks to cool down for about 1 minute
  • 7.Pack Snacks in a plastic bag and label it if required
  • 8.You are ready to enjoy!
